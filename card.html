Red-Tailed Hawk

Red-tailed hawks have amazing eyesight, and can even see into the ultraviolet spectrum!

  • Scientific Name: Buteo jamaicensis
  • Average Wingspan: 4 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Habitat: North America

The red-tailed hawk is the most common hawk in North America, and is characterized by its brick-red tail feathers. It feeds mainly on small mammals and reptiles. An efficient and capable flyer, red-tailed hawks can dive at speeds up to 120 mph!
